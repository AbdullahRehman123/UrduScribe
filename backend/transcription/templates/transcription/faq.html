Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about UrduScribe

UrduScribe is an AI-powered transcription service that converts audio files to text in both Urdu and English. Using advanced speech recognition technology, UrduScribe provides accurate transcriptions with speaker diarization (identifying different speakers in the audio).
UrduScribe offers flexible pricing plans. Contact us for detailed pricing information based on your usage needs. We offer both monthly subscriptions and pay-as-you-go options.

Absolutely. Your audio files and transcriptions are processed securely. We use encryption for data transmission and storage. Your files are automatically deleted after processing unless you choose to save them. We never share your data with third parties.
UrduScribe supports the most common audio formats including MP3, WAV, M4A, and more. If you have a file in an uncommon format, try converting it to MP3 or WAV first.
Yes! Once your transcription is complete, you can download it as a TXT file directly from the results page. Simply click the "Download" button to save your transcription to your device.
Currently, UrduScribe supports two languages: Urdu and English. We use state-of-the-art AI models specifically fine-tuned for these languages to ensure high accuracy. Support for additional languages is planned for future releases.
UrduScribe performs well with various accents and can handle moderate background noise. For best results, we recommend using clear audio recordings. However, our AI models are trained to be robust against common audio quality issues.
UrduScribe includes speaker diarization (speaker recognition) by default. When you upload your audio, simply select the number of speakers from the dropdown menu (1-4 speakers). The transcription will automatically label each speaker (SPEAKER_00, SPEAKER_01, etc.) in the results.
UrduScribe uses advanced AI models that provide high accuracy for both Urdu and English transcription. Accuracy depends on audio quality, speaker clarity, and accent. With clear audio, you can expect accuracy rates comparable to human transcription for most use cases.
